Phở Gà

Traditional Vietnamese Chicken Noodle Soup. A restorative, aromatic clear broth infused with charred ginger and spices.

local_fire_department 450 Kcal

Ingredients

Nutritional Info

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Prepare the Aromatic Base

Char the ginger and shallots over an open flame or under a broiler until slightly blackened. This provides the smoky, deep base for your broth. Rinse off any excess charred skin.

2

Simmer the Broth

Place chicken in a large pot, cover with water, and bring to a boil. Skim off impurities. Add charred ginger, shallots, and toasted spices. Simmer gently for 40 minutes.

3

Assemble and Serve

Remove chicken and shred the meat. Strain the broth and season with fish sauce. Soak noodles until tender. Arrange noodles and chicken in bowls, pour over hot broth, and top with herbs.
